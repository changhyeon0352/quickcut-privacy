Privacy Policy Quick Cut – Encoding with PC

Effective Date 2026-02-12
App Quick Cut – Encoding with PC
Platform Android (Google Play)

👋 Overview

Quick Cut – Encoding with PC is a mobile video editing utility that lets you plan video edits on your Android device and offload the actual encoding to your PC via an SMB network share.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is handled, why, and how.

📦 Data We Collect

This app does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers.

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No usage analytics are sent to us.
  • Video files and editing metadata stay on your device and your own PC.

🔒 Permissions Used

  • Storage (READ / WRITE) – Required to access and save video files on your device.
  • Network access – Used for SMB connection to your local PC and to load advertisements via Google AdMob.

All SMB transfers happen directly between your Android device and your own PC on your local network. No data passes through our servers.

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

Free-tier users see rewarded ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads in accordance with Google's own privacy policy.

  • Premium users do not see ads.
  • Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob data practices: support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

💳 In-App Purchases

Premium upgrade purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store any payment card information.

👶 Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top will always reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
